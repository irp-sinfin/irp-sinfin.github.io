Olivier Carton, Loïck Lhote, and Ignacio Mollo Cunnigham participated in the program on *Equidistribution: Arithmetic, Computational and Probabilistic Aspects* organized by Yann Bugeaud, Ted Slaman, Verónica Becher and Rober Tichy at the Institute fro Mathematical Sciences, University of Singapore, from 29 Apr - 11 May 2019. <https://ims.nus.edu.sg/events/2019/equi/index.php>
