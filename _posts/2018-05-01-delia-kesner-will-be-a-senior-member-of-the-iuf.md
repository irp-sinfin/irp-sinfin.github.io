Delia KESNER, professor at Université Paris Diderot, researcher at IRIF, and director of the LIA INFINIS [has been appointed as senior member of the Institut Universitaire de France as of October 1, 2018, for a period of five years](http://www.iufrance.fr/detail-de-lactualite/123.html). Researchers appointed at the Institut Universitaire de France are placed in a delegation position. They continue to work in their home institution and are discharged from two-thirds of their teaching service.
