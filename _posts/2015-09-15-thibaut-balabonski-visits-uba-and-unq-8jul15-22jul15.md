Thibaut Balabonski (Maître de conférences à l'[Université Paris Sud](http://www.u-psud.fr), [LRI](http://www.lri.fr), équipe [VALS](http://vals.lri.fr)) visited UBA and UNQ in the setting of an ECOS Sud collaboration project. He participated in discussions with Pablo Barenbaum and Eduardo Bonelli. He also gave a talk at the [LIA Workshop](/?p=726). Follow the links for further details.
