Flavia Bonomo presented an invited tutorial, “Generalized list matrix partition problems from the viewpoint of graph width parameters”, at the [27th Aussois Workshop on Combinatorial Optimization](https://cow25.sciencesconf.org/), Jan 6 - Jan 10, 2025, Aussois, France.
