title: "Award Ceremony for Raíces Prize to Delia Kesner &#8212; 6/Dec/2016"
---
The Award Ceremony for the Raíces Prize was held yesterday, 6 December 2016, at the Ministerio de Ciencia, Tecnología e Innovación Productiva, Buenos Aires, Argentina. Prof Delia Kesner, recipient of the award, was distinguished for her long and tireless dedication to fostering scientific collaboration between Argentina and France.
