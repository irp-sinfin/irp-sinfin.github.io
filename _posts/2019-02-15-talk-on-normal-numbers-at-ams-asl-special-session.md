Verónica Becher gave a talk on Normal numbers with digit dependencies at AMS-ASL Special Session on “Algorithmic Dimensions and Fractal Geometry” at the 2019 Joint Mathematics Meetings, January 16-19, 2019, Baltimore, MD, US. This is joint work with Olivier Carton and C. Aistleitner.
