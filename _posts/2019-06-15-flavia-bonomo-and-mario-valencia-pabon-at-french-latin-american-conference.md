Flavia Bonomo (UBA) and Mario Valencia Pabon (Université Paris 13), the two lead researchers of team Graph Theory and Combinatorial Optimization, have been invited to the graph session of the [French   
Latin-American Conference on New Trends in Applied Mathematics](http://eventos.cmm.uchile.cl/flacam2019/), which will be held at Santiago de Chile, from November 5 to November 8, 2019.
