The first QCOMICAL School will take place in Nancy, France, in 2025 as part of the QCOMICAL project. The school will offer tutorials and lectures by leading researchers involved in the project, working on quantum computing as well as related topics in programming languages and semantics, fostering interaction across foundational approaches. More information here: <https://qcomical2025.github.io>
