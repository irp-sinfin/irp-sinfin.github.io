title: "Gérald Tenenbaum will be at Buenos Aires in November, 2018"
---
[Prof. Gérald Tenenbaum](http://www.iecl.univ-lorraine.fr/~Gerald.Tenenbaum/) (Institut Élie Cartan, Faculté des Sciences, Université de Lorraine), recognised specialist in Analytic and Probabilistic Number Theory visits Exactas-UBA from the 13th to the 27th of November, 2018. He will give two talks at the Mathematics Department: - *On arithmetical processes*, on November 14.
- *On probabilistic models in number theory*, on November 15.
 
 Organises: Verónica Becher.
