Julien Clément and Loïck Lhote from Université de Caen will visit the Departamento de Computación (FCEyN - Universidad de Buenos Aires) and they will teach the course "Análisis de algoritmos" from October 26 to November 23. This course is devoted to undergraduated students or those who are interested in the principles of the probabilistic study of algorithms (For more information, [click here](https://campus.exactas.uba.ar/course/view.php?id=1320&section=0)).
