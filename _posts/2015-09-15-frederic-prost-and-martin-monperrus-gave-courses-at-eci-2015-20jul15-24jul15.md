Frédéric PROST (Associate Professor at [ LIG ](http://LIG.imag.fr), in the [CAPP](http://capp.imag.fr/) team) and Martin Monperrus (Associate professor at the [University of Lille](http://www.univ-lille1.fr/) (France) and a member of INRIA's [SPIRALS research group)](http://team.inria.fr/spirals) recently visited the Departamento de Computación (FCEyN, UBA) and gave courses at the winter school in Computer Science [ECI 2015](http://www.dc.uba.ar/events/eci/2015/cursos).
