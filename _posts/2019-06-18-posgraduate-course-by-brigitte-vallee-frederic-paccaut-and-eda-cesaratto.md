From the 15th to the 24th of October 2019, Eda Cesaratto, Frédéric Paccaut, and Brigitte Vallée will give a postgraduate course titled "Sistemas dinámicos en teoría de números" at the Department of Mathematics in Universidad Nacional del Sur.
