Two new publications in collaboration between Argentina and France have been accepted at the 15th International Symposium on Functional and Logic Programming ([FLOPS 2020](https://www.ipl.riec.tohoku.ac.jp/FLOPS2020/)).  
  
Antonio Bucciarelli, Delia Kesner, Alejandro Ríos, and Andrés Viso. "The Bang Calculus Revisited".  
  
Beniamino Accattoli and Alejandro Díaz-Caro. "The Distributive λ-Calculus".
