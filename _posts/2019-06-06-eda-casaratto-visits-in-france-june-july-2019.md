title: "Eda Casaratto visits in France &#8211; June/July 2019"
---
Eda Cesaratto is invited on a "Chair d'Excellence" inside ALENOR project by the AMACC team at the GREYC Laboratory in the University of Caen from June 9 to July 8, 2019. She is going to give a talk about "Dynamical analysis of particular trajectories for the Euclid system" at LMRS laboratory in Rouen. Also, she is visiting IRIF at Paris VII to work within the ANR Project CODYS, and is participating of the AOFA'2019 International Conference.
