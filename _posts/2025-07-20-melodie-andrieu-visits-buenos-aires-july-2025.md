Mélodie Andrieu, CNRS and University of the Littoral Opal Coast, France, in delegation in Center for Mathematical Modeling (CMM), at Universidad de Chile, visits Buenos Aires 26 to 29 July 2025 to work with Verónica Becher and the group KAPOW.
