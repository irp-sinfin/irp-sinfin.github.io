Marthe Bonamy (CNRS and Université de Bordeaux), Flavia Bonomo (Universidad de Buenos Aires), and Maya Stein (Universidad de Chile) are the directors of the new Math-AmSud project "*STALGRAPH: Structural and algorithmic graph theory*". Several members of the LIA SINFIN are involved in the project.
