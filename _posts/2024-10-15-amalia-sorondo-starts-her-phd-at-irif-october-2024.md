Amalia Sorondo will begin her PhD at IRIF in October 2024, funded by the École Doctorale de Sciences Mathématiques de Paris-Centre. She will work on ordered graphs under the supervision of Pierre Charbit and Michel Habib, in collaboration with Flavia Bonomo.
