title: "Alejandro Díaz-Caro visits LSV and LRI, March 26 &#8211; April 7, 2018"

From March 26 to April 7, Alejandro Díaz-Caro (UNQ/CONICET), will be visiting the LSV and LRI at Université Paris-Saclay (Cachan and Orsay) to work with Gilles Dowek and Benoît Valiron. Previously, Alejandro Díaz-Caro will be at giving a seminar at the [Workshop on Combining Viewpoints in Quantum Theory](http://homepages.inf.ed.ac.uk/cheunen/cvqt/) in Edinburgh (March 19-22).
