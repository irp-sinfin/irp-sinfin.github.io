Beniamino Accattoli will give the course "λ-Calculus and reasonable cost models" at the [Escuela de Ciencias Informáticas (ECI)](https://eci2019.dc.uba.ar/index.html), founded by Inria as sponsor of the school. The school is being held at Universidad de Buenos Aires bethen the 22th and the 26th of July, 2019.
