title: "Pablo Rotondo visits Buenos Aires, July-August, 2025"
---
Pablo Rotondo visits DC/ICC University of Buenos Aires, July 27 to August 8, 2025. He will give the course on Probabilistic Analysis of Algorithms at Escuela de Ciencias Informáticas, and then work with Eda Cesaratto and other members of the KAPOW group.
