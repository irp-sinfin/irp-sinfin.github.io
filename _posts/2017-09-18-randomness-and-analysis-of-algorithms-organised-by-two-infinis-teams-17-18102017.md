The meeting "Randomness and Analysis of Algorithms" will take place in the Departamento de Computación, Facultad de Ciencias Exactas y Naturales, UBA on October 17 and 18, 2017. It is organised by the project [Alea En Amsud](http://www.ungs.edu.ar/dynalco/?page_id=938) (STIC AMSUD 2016-2017) and the following teams of INFINIS Lab

- [Algorithmic Randomness, Kolmogorov Complexity, Problems on Words](http://www-2.dc.uba.ar/profesores/becher/infinis.html)
- [Combinatorics and Analysis of Algorithms in Number Theory, Information Theory and Cryptography](http://www.ungs.edu.ar/dynalco/?page_id=448&lang=en)

This meeting will precede the INFINIS WORKSHOP that will be held on October 19 also in the Departamento de Computación, Facultad de Ciencias Exactas y Naturales, UBA. **For more information, please, visit the meeting's webpage: [Randomness and Analysis of Algorithms](http://www.ungs.edu.ar/dynalco/?p=1216&lang=en).**
