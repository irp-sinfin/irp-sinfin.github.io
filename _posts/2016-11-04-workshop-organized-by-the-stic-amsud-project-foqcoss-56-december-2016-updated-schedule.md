**FoQCoSS Kickoff Workshop** *Foundations of Quantum Computation: Syntax and Semantics,* 5-6 December, 2016 Universidad Nacional de Quilmes Buenos Aires, Argentina <http://diaz-caro.web.unq.edu.ar/foqcoss.html>Under the STIC-AmSud project FoQCoSS, a workshop with presentations by its members will be held on December 5th and 6th in Buenos Aires (Argentina) at the campus of the Universidad Nacional de Quilmes. Please, confirm attendance by sending your name and affiliation to [alejandro@diaz-caro.info ](mailto:alejandro@diaz-caro.info)At the bottom of this email, you will find the program.
