On September 13, 2023, from 13 to 15h, French time (8 to 10h Argentinian time), we will have a general meeting. All the team members are invited.

The meeting will also be attended by representatives from our supporting institutions.

During this meeting, the team leaders will present their achivements in the period 2019-2023, and their projects for 2024-2028.
