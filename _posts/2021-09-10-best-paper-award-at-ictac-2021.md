Alejandro Díaz-Caro (Argentina) and Gilles Dowek (France), got the "Best Paper Award" at the 18th International Colloquium on Theoretical Aspects of Computing (ICTAC 2021) for their work "[A New Connective in Natural Deduction, and Its Application to Quantum Computing](https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-030-85315-0_11)".
