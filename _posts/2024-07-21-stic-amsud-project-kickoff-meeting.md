The kickoff meeting for the STIC-AMSUD project "Entropy and Probabilistic Analysis in Algorithms," directed by Eda Cesaratto and Pablo Rotondo, will take place from October 21-25, 2024. Participants from Argentina, France, Chile, and Uruguay will meet at the Departamento de Computación, Facultad de Ciencias Exactas y Naturales, Universidad de Buenos Aires, and ICC CONICET. More details are available [here](https://raofa-sinfin.greyc.fr/amsud-entropy-and-probabilistic-analysis-in-algorithms/).
