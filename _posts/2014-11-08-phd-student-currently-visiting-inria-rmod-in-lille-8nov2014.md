The PhD student Guido Chari (DC, FCEyN, UBA) is visiting the INRIA RMOD Laboratory at Lille from 15/October until 10/November. He is working on collaboration with Dr Stephane Ducasse (thesis co-supervisor), Marcus Denker and Stefan Marr.
