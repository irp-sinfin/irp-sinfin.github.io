Verónica Becher participated in the International Conference on "Prime numbers and arithmetic randomness”, Centre International de Rencontres Mathématiques (CIRM) , Marseille, Junio 23-25, 2025.  
She talked about "A simply nomal number with digit dependencies”, joint work between Verónica Becher (UBA), Gérald Tenenbaum (CNRS Institut Élie Cartan de Lorraine, France), Agustín Marchionna (UBA).
