Raúl Fervari (Universidad Nacional de Córdoba &amp; CONICET) visits Stéphane Demri at LSV, ENS Paris-Cachan from August 31 to September 12, 2018. From this visit, a joint publication entitled “On the complexity of modal separation logics” has been issued.
