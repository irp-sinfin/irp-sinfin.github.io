</div>**Title:** On the Completeness of a Syntactically Linear Logic

**Abstract:** The L<sup>S</sup> calculus, an extension of the proof language of linear logic related to quantum computing, was given a concrete semantics in [\[DCM23\]](https://arxiv.org/abs/2205.02142v2). In this thesis, we prove completeness results for this semantics with respect to computational equivalence. We introduce an operational semantics for L<sup>S</sup> and show its isomorphism with the categorical semantics under certain conditions. Moreover, we show that the category can be restricted to finitely generated semimodules, which possess better properties.
