Alejandro Díaz-Caro (Argentina), Malena Ivnisky (Argentina), Hernán Melgratti (Argentina), and Benoît Valiron (France) got their work "**[A finite-dimensional model for affine, linear quantum lambda calculi with general recursion](https://mivnisky.github.io/qfixpoint.pdf)**" accepted for presentation at [TYPES (27th International Conference on Types for Proofs and Programs)](https://types21.liacs.nl/) and at [WiL (5th Workshop on Women in Logic)](https://sites.google.com/g.uporto.pt/wil2021).

Malena is presenting at both venues.
