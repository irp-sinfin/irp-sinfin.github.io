Raúl Fervari has been awarded the Jean D'Alembert Fellowship for 2024-2026 by Université Paris-Saclay. He will join the LMF for six months next year.

More details on the fellowship can be found [here](https://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/en/research/research-programmes/jean-dalembert-fellowship-program).
