From the 15th to the 24th of October 2019, Brigitte Vallée, Frédéric Paccaut and Eda Cesaratto will give a postgraduate course titled "Sistemas dinámicos en teoría de números" at the Department of Mathematics in Universidad Nacional del Sur.
