<div style="text-align: center;">## Logic and Foundations of Programming Languages Day

 **May 21, 2018 Departamento de Computación, FCEN, Universidad de Buenos Aires Pabellón 1, Ciudad Universitaria, Buenos Aires** </div> The [LoReL team](https://sites.google.com/site/lorellabs/) and the Laboratoire International Associé INFINIS organize a day on logic and foundations of programming languages. The intention is to bring together researchers from the Río de la Plata region. We will profit from the visit of several French colleagues who will be in the region on that date.
