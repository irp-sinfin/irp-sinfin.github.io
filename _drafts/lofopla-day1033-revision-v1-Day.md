<div style="text-align: center;">## Day on Logic and Foundations of Programming Language @ INFINIS

 May 21, 2018 Departamento de Computación, FCEN, Universidad de Buenos Aires Pabellón 1, Ciudad Universitaria, Buenos Aires </div> The LoReL team and the Laboratoire International Associé INFINIS organize a day on logic and foundations of programming language. The intention is to bring together researchers in the Río de la Plata region. We will profit from several visit from French colleagues who will be in the region on that date. Early list of confirmed speakers: - Verónica Becher (Universidad de Buenos Aitres / CONICET)
- Olivier Carton (Université Paris Diderot)
- Alejandro Díaz-Caro (ICC-CONICET /Universidad Nacional de Quilmes)
- Santiago Figueira (Universidad de Buenos Aires / CONICET)
- Mauricio Guillermo (Universidad de la República)
- Delia Kesner (Université Paris Diderot)
