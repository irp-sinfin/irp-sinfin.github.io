A publication in collaboration between Argentina and France has been accepted at the *31st International Conference on Probabilistic, Combinatorial and Asymptotic Methods for the Analysis of Algorithms* ([AofA2020](https://www.math.aau.at/AofA2020/)):  
  
Valérie Berthé, Eda Cesaratto, Frédéric Paccaut, Pablo Rotondo, Martín Safe and Brigitte Vallée. "Two arithmetical sources and their associated tries". LIPIcs 159(4), 2020. <http://doi.org/10.4230/LIPIcs.AofA.2020.4>
