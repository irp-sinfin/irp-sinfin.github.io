On the occasion of the visit of Delia Kesner, director from the French side of SINFIN, and Olivier Carton, from the Randomness and Analysis of Algorithms team, we have organised this mini-workshop on Monday, March 11, 2024, with the following program.

**18.00-18.30 - Team: [Logics and Dynamics of Programming Languages](https://sinfin-ldpl.github.io/)**  
Talk #1: Linear logic as a key to understand programs  
Speaker: [Pablo Barenbaum](https://foones.github.io/) 🇦🇷  
Joint work with [Delia Kesner](https://www.irif.fr/~kesner/) 🇫🇷, [Mariana Milicich](https://www.irif.fr/users/milicich/index) 🇫🇷, and [Eduardo Bonelli](https://ebonelli.github.io/) 🇺🇸

Talk #2: A polymorphic extension of a linear proof language for intuitionistic linear logic  
Speaker: [Malena Ivnisky](https://mivnisky.github.io/) 🇦🇷  
Co-authors: [Alejandro Díaz-Caro](https://staff.dc.uba.ar/adiazcaro/) 🇦🇷, [Gilles Dowek](http://www.lsv.fr/~dowek/) 🇫🇷, and Octavio Malherbe 🇺🇾

**18.35-19.05 - Team: [Graph Theory and Combinatorial Optimization](https://www-2.dc.uba.ar/grupinv/grafos/sinfin.htm)**  
Talk: Parameterized algorithms for thinness via the cluster module number  
Speaker: Eric Brandwein 🇦🇷  
Co-authors: [Flavia Bonomo](https://www-2.dc.uba.ar/staff/fbonomo/) 🇦🇷 and [Ignasi Sau](https://www.lirmm.fr/~sau/) 🇫🇷

**19.10-19.40 - Team: [Theory of Structured Data](https://sites.google.com/view/theoryofstructureddata-sinfin/home)**  
Talk: First Order Logic with unary negation and transitive closure  
Speaker: [Santiago Figueira](http://www.glyc.dc.uba.ar/santiago/) 🇦🇷  
Co-authors: [Diego Figueira](http://www.labri.fr/~dfigueir) 🇫🇷 and Edwin Pin 🇦🇷

**19.45-20.15 - Team: [Randomness and Analysis of Algorithms](https://raofa-sinfin.greyc.fr/)**  
Talk: Nested perfect arrays  
Speaker: [Olivier Carton](https://www.irif.fr/~carton/) 🇫🇷  
Co-author: [Verónica Becher](https://www-2.dc.uba.ar/staff/becher/) 🇦🇷
