The International Research Project SINFIN was created in 2019 as a continuation of the Laboratoire International Associé INFINIS (2011-2019). The institutions involved are:

- [Institut de Recherche en Informatique Fondamentale](https://www.irif.fr/) - [Université Paris-Cité](https://u-paris.fr/)
- [CNRS](http://www.cnrs.fr/)
- [Instituto de Ciencias de la Computación](https://icc.fcen.uba.ar/) - [Universidad de Buenos Aires](https://exactas.uba.ar/)
- [CONICET](https://www.conicet.gov.ar)
